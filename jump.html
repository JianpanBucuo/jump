By entering the game, each participant unconditionally agrees to these Terms and Conditions.

The Prizes

After participants finish the game, those who share their scores on Facebook or Twitter will be eligible for the lucky draw of prizes. All submissions should be publicly posted on participant's Facebook or Twitter timeline, with the hashtag #Honor10.

i. Participants with the score between 100 and 515: Participants who share their score on Facebook or Twitter and tag 2 friends will have the chance of winning an Honor AM51 Bluetooth speaker. 3 winners will be chosen. If chosen, their 2 friends will each win a pair of Honor AM175 hybrid earphones.

ii. Participants with the score more than 515: Participants who share their score on Facebook or Twitter and tag 2 friends will have the chance of winning an Honor 10 (4GB RAM + 128GB ROM, random color). 1 winner will be chosen. If chosen, the 2 friends will each win a pair of Honor AM175 hybrid earphones.

Honor will cover all duty tax and courier charge for prize shipping. In a case where the prize is not delivered due to winners' reasons, it will be regarded that the winner has given up his right to claim prize.

Honor reserves the right to exclude a participant who violates the conditions of participation or tampers with the competition. If a participant is excluded, prizes may be subsequently reclaimed by a new winner.
